# About Me
I am now a Ph.D. Candidate at [School of Civil Engineering](http://civil.bjtu.edu.cn/), [Beijing Jiaotong University](https://bjtu.edu.cn/). My research interests include **X-ray Computed Tomography**, **Carbon Neutrality Science and Engineering**, and **Structural Health Monitoring**.
